1. What is sensor?

Sensor or input is used to detect something!



A sensor is an object whose purpose is to detect events or changes in its environment and sends the information to the computer which then tells the actuator (output devices) to provide the corresponding output.A sensor detects and responds to some type of input from the physical environment. Some examples of the sensors are: light, heat, motion, moisture, pressure, or any one of a great number of other environmental phenomena.

Everyday Objects that Use Sensors!


Light turns on automatically at night when the LIGHT SENSOR detects less LIGHT

Door turns on automatically when the PROXIMITY SENSOR detects SOMETHING NEAR.

Air conditioner turns on automatically when the TEMPERATURE SENSOR detects higher room temperature.
